Tasting Menu Courses

1. Mushroom Salad

A delicate medley of earthy mushrooms, paired with silky fish cake, crisp flat beans, and toasted nuts, all drizzled with tamari.

2. Salmon Nigiri

A one-bite sensation of perfectly seared salmon atop a vinegared rice base, crowned with a torched garlic crunch for an exquisite balance of smokiness and umami.

3. Charred Eel Duo

Tender, charred eel glazed to perfection, served with a refreshing tang of pickled daikon radish, all elevated by a complementary glaze for a burst of flavors in every bite.

4. Chashu Pork Nigiri

Smoked chashu pork on a vinegared rice bed, adorned with seaweed strings and garnished with vibrant micro-flowers, offering a fusion of rich, smoky flavors and delicate textures.

5. Wagyu Tataki

Seared wagyu tataki served with a tangy citrus ponzu sauce, peppered with arugula, cherry tomatoes, rich cheese, and toasted pine nuts, creating an elegant symphony of flavors and textures.

6. Braised Tuna Roll

Succulent braised tuna placed atop a delicate tuna roll, sprinkled with black & white sesame, crunchy peanuts, and fresh microgreens, delivering a delightful contrast of flavors and textures.

7. Sashimi Elegance Platter

An opulent selection of sashimi, featuring A5 wagyu with caviar, A5 wagyu with salmon roe, and a selection of pristine fish: hamachi, snapper, sea bass, albacore, accompanied by scallions, pickled ginger, wasabi, and citrus ponzu sauce, topped with puffed rice for an added crunch.

8. Blackberry Mochi

A delicate, sweet treat of soft mochi, filled with creamy vanilla and a luscious blackberry compote, providing a perfect balance of sweetness and tartness in every bite.

9. Flowered Seaweed Crunch

A dark chocolate crust, textured to resemble seaweed, paired with a fragrant rose-strawberry whipped cream shaped into a rose, creating a luxurious and whimsical end to your meal.
